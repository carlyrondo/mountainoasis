Local Attractions

While there's plenty to do on-site at the Mountain Oasis Bed & Breakfast, there's no way you will be bored with all of the local attractions in Grand Lake, Colorado. These are just some of the nearby places you may find yourself looking to go!

Shopping

The Grand Avenue Boardwalk

You can't visit Grand Lake without taking a walk down the Grand Avenue Boardwalk! With a variety of shops, anyone could find something they like here.

Entertainment

Rocky Mountain Repertory Theatre

See a professional live show at Grand Lake's theatre where they put on musicals and plays.

Seasonal Festival

From 4th of July fiewworks to the Winter Carnvial, get out and experience the variety of local seasonal festivals!

Scenery

Trail Ridge Road

Get on the road and take a drive through Rocky Mountain National Park! You'll find stunning views you can't see anywhere else!

Restuarants

Historic Rapids Lodge & Restaurant

An upscale dining experience true to the mountains. Here, you can eat like a local and try their classic elk chops.
