Frequently Asked Questions

What is the pet policy?

We do not allow guests to bring their pets. Service animals are the only exception.

How long do guests usually stay?

The average guest stays for 2 nights, however we do have the ocassonial week long guests.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes! We offer 10% off to military and seniors with provided identification.

What accomdations does the accessible room have?

To ensure a comfortable stay for all guests we have the accesible room on the main floor for easy wheelchair access and general mobility accomodations. There are also hearing and visual accomodations in this room as well.

Is there onsite parking?

Yes! Free onsite parking in included in your stay.We allow cars and trailers. RV's and trailers cannot be parked in our lot due to parking ordinances.

Is this family friendly lodging?

Most of our guests are adults, and the experience is tailored to an adult oasis. However, we understand that you can't always leave your little ones behind, so we accodomate families with children in our Family Suite.
