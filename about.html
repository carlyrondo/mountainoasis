Our Story

Mr. Doppelganger immigrated to the US as a child with his parents. They opened their first bed and breakfast in 1980. Inspired by his parents, Mr. Doppelganger kept the family business alive by opening his own bed & breakfasts. Today, he runs the b&b's with his wife Frances, and two daughters, Faith and Emma. They have 3 other bed and breakfast locations. in other cities including Erie, PA, Conneaut Lake, OH, and Put-in-Bay, OH. All 4 locations are located in small towns with beautiful scenery and a vibrant community.


This Location

Mountain Oasis Bed & Breakfast is family owned and run log cabin style house that makes you feel right at home. Tucked quaintly in the woods of Grand Lake, Colorado, we have several rooms available for rent. It is the perfect place to get away and get in touch with nature. Room rentals include access to the lake nearby, for the ultimate relaxing getaway. While we are located in the woods of Grand Lake, surrounded by the beauty of Colorado nature, there are nearby towns for plenty of shopping,restaurants, attraction, etc.
